Welcome to
the MFA D+T report!

For the past 3 months I have been gathering information about the MFA Design + Technology (D+T) community to try to learn a about the alumni and current students. By launching a quick survey, I gathered a sample of 120 responses containing data on their nationalities and where they are living now, what industries they are working in and what trends in industries of design and technology they think should be studied and explored in the program.

Here below I present a series of data visualizations based on the data gathered and created with the Javascript library D3.js. This project also includes code in HTML, CSS and vanilla Javascript. I hope you find this information insightful. Enjoy!
