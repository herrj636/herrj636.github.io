Covid-19 Visualization by Territorial Empathy

Project Overview

We believe there's a correlation between community behavior and their exposure to the virus. Therefore, we want to analyze the impact of the virus on these communities, and whether they are adhering to the social distancing rules. If not, we want to better understand the factors leading to non-compliance, which may include socio-economic status, race, immigration status and gender.
